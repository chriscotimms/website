Who I am

I'm a Front-End Web Developer with an interest in Accessibility. This website is based upon the approach established in Phsychic Refuge, a collaborative project focused on interactive access settings by Studio Hyte and artist Sophie Hoyle. The webpage, similar in approach to Studio Hyte's, allows users to toggle through a choice of visual settings, with the intention of improving access.
